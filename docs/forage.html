Lakewood Walking Tour

6. Forage Public House

Head south out of Lakewood Park and up Belle Avenue where you can enjoy walking past the stately homes on this street. End your walking tour with a meal at Forage Public House. The fried cauliflower appetizer and burgers are very tasty. If the weather is warm, you can sit on the shaded patio.
