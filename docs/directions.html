Lakewood Walking Tour

2. Lion Statue

  • Head west down Detroit Avenue, Lakewood's main business district. You will walk past Lakewood Public Library, Beat Cycles and the Masonic Temple. Turn right on Brockley Avenue and cross the railroad tracks and Clifton Boulevard. At the southwest corner of Brockley and Lake Avenue, you can admire the lion statues in front of a private residence.
