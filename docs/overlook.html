Lakewood Walking Tour

4. Overlook Park

You will emerge from the pedestrian path onto Edgewater Drive. Here you will find gorgeous mansions facing Lake Erie. Go east/left on Edgewater and turn right onto Webb Road. At the end of Webb is a mini-park with two benches offering a view of the lake. You will be on top of a cliff overlooking the lake behind a chain link fence - no beach here. The next stop on the walking tour will bring you closer to the lake.
